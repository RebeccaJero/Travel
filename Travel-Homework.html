    • About Hawaii

    Hawaii is a volcanic chain of islands that consists of 137 islands and atolls.
    Further, of these, eight are inhabited and six are open to tourists. Hawaiʻi “The Big Island”.
    Maui “The Valley Isle”.
    Oʻahu “The Gathering Place”. Kauaʻi “The Garden Isle”.
    Molokaʻi “The Friendly Isle”.
    Lānaʻi “The Pineapple Isle”.
    Niʻihau “The Forbidden Isle” and Kahoʻolawe “The Target Isle”.
    All the Hawaiian Islands have beautiful beaches, a delightful nature, an amazing underwater world, their own sights and of course their own charm.
